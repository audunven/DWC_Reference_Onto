Ontology source

Abstract

The DWC Reference Ontology is an OWL ontology developed in Work Packge 4 of the Digital Water City project. Data requirements collected from the Paris and Milan cases in the project have been mapped to the SAREF suite of ontologies developed by the Smart M2M Technical Committee in ETSI. A few extensions have been identified. These extensions are defined using the namespace "https://www.digital-water.city/dwc-reference-ontology/" to separate them from concepts in the re-used ontologies from SAREF.
